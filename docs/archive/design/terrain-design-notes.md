# Terrain Design Notes — All 16 Missions

> Every map should be as handcrafted as the mission that takes place on it.
> — design directive

## Design Principles

1. **Chokepoints create tactical decisions** — bridges, narrow passes, gaps in mangroves
2. **Resources require expansion** — safe fish near base, rich salvage deep in enemy territory
3. **Flanking paths reward creativity** — alternative routes through water (Raftsmen), tall grass (stealth)
4. **Defensive terrain is finite** — walls and towers can't cover everything
5. **The map teaches the mechanic** — mission terrain should guide players toward the intended play pattern

## Mission-by-Mission Assessment

### M1: Beachhead (48×44)
**Purpose**: Tutorial — teach gather/build/train
**Terrain**: Beach landing zone south, river east-west through middle, mangrove grove west (timber), salvage northeast
**Assessment**: GOOD — simple layout, resources clearly visible, river creates natural boundary
**Needs**: Verify bridge crossing is obvious. Salvage area should feel like "bonus optional zone."

### M2: The Causeway (48×30)
**Purpose**: Escort — defend convoy along a road
**Terrain**: Dense jungle north/south flanking a causeway, river crossings at 3 chokepoints
**Assessment**: GOOD — linear map forces convoy route, ambush points are clear
**Needs**: Ensure ambush positions have cover for Scale-Guard but are visible once scouted.

### M3: Firebase Delta (40×44)
**Purpose**: Capture and hold 3 hilltops
**Terrain**: Triangle of elevated positions with valleys between
**Assessment**: GOOD — natural triangle creates the multi-front challenge
**Needs**: Paths between hilltops should be narrow enough to force garrison decisions.

### M4: Prison Break (32×28)
**Purpose**: Stealth infiltration
**Terrain**: Dense compound with detection towers, tall grass approach from SE
**Assessment**: GOOD — small map forces close quarters, tall grass creates cover lanes
**Needs**: Detection tower placement should create visible "safe corridors" between them.

### M5: Siphon Valley (56×48)
**Purpose**: Destroy 3 siphons with Sappers
**Terrain**: River valley running north-south, siphons at west bend / north bridges / east coast
**Assessment**: GOOD — wide map allows build-up, three distinct target areas
**Needs**: Each siphon should have a different approach challenge (river crossing, bridge, coast).

### M6: Monsoon Ambush (48×40)
**Purpose**: 8-wave base defense
**Terrain**: Pre-built base center, 4 approach roads from cardinal directions
**Assessment**: NEEDS WORK — the "monsoon" is no longer visual weather, so terrain should reflect it.
**Action**: Add mud patches along approach roads that slow movement. Add flooding zones that activate via triggers at wave 4.

### M7: River Rats (52×40)
**Purpose**: Capture the flag — retrieve 5 supply crates
**Terrain**: Wide river dividing map, 2 bridges, crates on east bank
**Assessment**: GOOD — river is the core mechanic, bridge contests create tension
**Needs**: Ensure dock-building locations on the west bank are near the river edge.

### M8: Underwater Cache (36×32)
**Purpose**: Rescue Cpl. Splash, dive for cache
**Terrain**: Lake center, outpost southeast, extraction northwest
**Assessment**: GOOD for a small stealth mission
**Needs**: Lake should be prominently visible as the objective area.

### M9: Dense Canopy (52×44)
**Purpose**: Fog recon — find intel markers
**Terrain**: Thick mangrove coverage reducing vision, open clearings for ambushes
**Assessment**: GOOD — fog + dense terrain creates atmosphere
**Needs**: Intel markers should be in clearings that are findable but require scouting.

### M10: The Healer's Grove (56×48)
**Purpose**: Destroy 4 fuel depots
**Terrain**: North industrial zone with fuel tanks, southern approach through forest
**Assessment**: GOOD — clear target area in the north
**Needs**: Multiple approach paths to each fuel tank for tactical variety.

### M11: Entrenchment (48×48)
**Purpose**: 12-wave island defense
**Terrain**: Central island with causeways from all sides
**Assessment**: NEEDS WORK — tidal mechanics described in briefing but terrain doesn't change.
**Action**: Add low-ground "tidal flat" zones that become water tiles at wave 4 and 8 via triggers.

### M12: The Stronghold (36×44)
**Purpose**: Rescue Sgt. Fang from fortified compound
**Terrain**: Clifftop position, layered walls, narrow approach
**Assessment**: GOOD — vertical design creates escalation
**Needs**: Southern extraction route should be the "fighting retreat" path.

### M13: Supply Lines (64×56)
**Purpose**: Multi-base assault on Scale-Guard HQ
**Terrain**: Largest map — multiple bases, supply routes, central Great Siphon compound
**Assessment**: GOOD for scope but complex
**Needs**: Supply route connections between player bases should be defensible but long.

### M14: Gas Depot / Iron Delta (60×52)
**Purpose**: Naval island capture
**Terrain**: River channels between islands, each island has a capture point
**Assessment**: GOOD — water traversal is the core mechanic
**Needs**: Islands should be clearly separated with narrow channel crossings.

### M15: Serpent's Lair (52×52)
**Purpose**: Breach citadel, fight Ironjaw
**Terrain**: 3 defensive rings (outer, middle, inner), toxic moat around citadel
**Assessment**: GOOD — concentric ring design creates escalation
**Needs**: Each ring breach should feel like progress. Inner ring should feel claustrophobic.

### M16: The Reckoning (64×64)
**Purpose**: 10-wave defense then counterattack
**Terrain**: Player base south, enemy base north, contested middle ground
**Assessment**: GOOD — largest map, two-phase design
**Needs**: Clear "frontline" in the middle that shifts northward during counterattack.

## Summary: Missions Needing Terrain Updates

|---------|-------|--------|

| M11 | Tidal mechanics described but not implemented in terrain | Add low-ground zones that become water via triggers |
