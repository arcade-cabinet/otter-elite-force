# Lore Bible — The Copper-Silt Reach

## Setting

The Copper-Silt Reach is a humid river-jungle war zone of mangroves, crossings, settlements, depots, mud flats, and fortified chokepoints. Before the occupation, otters and reptiles traded freely along the waterways. Now the channels are fortified, the villages are garrisoned, and the Reach is a war zone.

## Factions

### Otter Elite Force (OEF) — Player Faction
Guerrilla fighters reclaiming their homeland. Mobile, resourceful, outgunned but never outmatched. Fight from lodges (field HQs), harvest fish and timber, build light fortifications.

**Units:** River Rat (worker), Mudfoot (infantry), Shellcracker (heavy), Sapper (engineer), Raftsman (water transport), Mortar Otter (artillery), Diver (stealth/water)

### Scale-Guard — Enemy Faction
Entrenched reptilian occupiers. Industrial, fortified, numerous. Control chokepoints, siphon resources, garrison every crossing.

**Units:** Skink (scout), Gator (infantry), Viper (ranged), Snapper (heavy), Scout Lizard (recon), Croc Champion (elite), Siphon Drone (tech), Serpent King (boss)

## Campaign Arc

**Chapter 1: First Landing** — Establish beachhead, escort convoys, capture hilltops, rescue Gen. Whiskers
**Chapter 2: Deep Operations** — Destroy siphons, survive monsoon waves, river interdiction, rescue Cpl. Splash
**Chapter 3: Turning Tide** — Fog recon, scorched earth, tidal siege, rescue Sgt. Fang
**Chapter 4: Final Offensive** — Assault HQ, island campaign, boss fight vs Ironjaw, final reckoning

## Terminology

Prefer these terms in all player-facing text:
- **Otter Elite Force / OEF** (not "URA" in player-facing copy)
- **Scale-Guard** (not "reptiles" or "enemies")
- **Copper-Silt Reach** (the theater of war)
- **Lodge** (player's field HQ, not "base" or "command center")
- **Captain** (the player, not "commander" or "player")
